About me

I like math. And how to use it to address foundational problems in physics and computer science. I work in operational reconstructions of quantum theory, the quantum-to-classical transition, non-Kolmogorovian probability theory, alternative axiomatisations of general relativity, indefinite causality in quantum mechanics, new semantics for higher-order computation and the origin of spacetime. I am a PhD student in the Dakic group, in the University of Vienna.
